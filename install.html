Step by step

How to install MedSaathi

1
Open MedSaathi in Chrome
Open your Chrome browser and go to the MedSaathi website link. Make sure you're using Chrome — not Samsung browser.
2
Tap the 3 dots menu ⋮
In the top right corner of Chrome, tap the three vertical dots to open the menu.
⋮ → Menu appears at bottom of screen
3
Tap "Add to Home screen"
Scroll down in the menu and tap "Add to Home screen". A popup will appear with the MedSaathi name.
📲 "Add to Home screen" option in menu
4
Tap "Add" — Done! 🎉
Confirm by tapping "Add". MedSaathi icon will appear on your home screen instantly. Tap it to open like any app!
1
Open MedSaathi in Safari
Open Safari (not Chrome) on your iPhone and go to the MedSaathi website link. This only works in Safari on iPhone.
2
Tap the Share button
At the bottom of Safari, tap the Share button — it looks like a box with an arrow pointing upward ↑
⬆️ Share button at the bottom of Safari
3
Tap "Add to Home Screen"
Scroll down in the share sheet and tap "Add to Home Screen". You'll see a preview of the MedSaathi icon.
➕ "Add to Home Screen" in share sheet
4
Tap "Add" — Done! 🎉
Tap "Add" in the top right. MedSaathi icon appears on your home screen. Open it and it runs fullscreen like a real app!
